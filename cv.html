Research highlights
...

Semantic primitives NLP
I showed that the semantic primitives are not capture in special places in word embeddings of early language models [2].
Foundations of cognitive science
We showed that, contrary to the common narrative about the foundations of cognitive science, the foundations were long in the making, especially in the Europe, through the work of Mach, Piaget, and other great thinkers [1].
Inverse semigroups and partial (local) symmetry
We showed that algorithm for building partial automorphism inverse monoid of a graph can be reduced to coloring that corresponds to 1-WL, except in the cases which are 1-WL indistinguishable [3 and current research]. Moreover, we found graphs that have only trivial automorphism group on each level up to 6 vertex-induced subgraphs.
